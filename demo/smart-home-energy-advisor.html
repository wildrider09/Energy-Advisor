⚡ Smart Home Energy Advisor

AI-Powered Energy Intelligence for Amazon Smart Thermostat (Snyder)

Project Overview

A complete end-to-end smart home energy management system built for the Amazon Smart Thermostat.

🌡️ HVAC Simulation

Physics-based thermostat simulator generates realistic events every 2 seconds. Models temperature drift, compressor cycles, aux heater activation, fan operation, and DR lifecycle. Uses real Bangalore weather via Open-Meteo API.

🔍 Anomaly Detection

Real-time engine evaluates device state on every tick. Detects 5 categories: aux heater overuse, filter degradation, runtime spikes, DR preparation needs, and connectivity loss. Each anomaly includes description and recommendation.

🤖 AI-Powered Chat

Claude on Bedrock receives full device context — state, events, anomalies, weather — and responds in homeowner-friendly language with dollar estimates, time projections, and specific actions.

📊 Live Dashboard

Real-time web dashboard: indoor/outdoor temp, HVAC status, runtime bars, energy costs, DR state. Polls every 1.5s for state, every 3s for proactive alerts.

⚡ Demand Response

Complete 5-stage lifecycle: SCHEDULED → PRE_COOLING → ACTIVE → RECOVERY → NONE. 3-4 events per week. Auto-adjusts setpoints to reduce grid load while maintaining comfort.

🎛️ AI Optimization

Every 60s, Bedrock analyzes state + weather + forecast + trends + preferences → returns JSON {mode, setpoint, reason}. Users can override; AI learns from manual adjustments.

The 3 Intelligence Pillars

Every feature in the app maps to one of three core intelligence pillars.

🔧 Pillar 1: HVAC Health Intelligence

Continuously monitors HVAC system health by analyzing component behavior, runtime patterns, and maintenance indicators. Detects problems before they become expensive failures.

Anomaly Detectors

Auxiliary Heater Overuse — Triggers when aux heater runs 3+ times/day while outdoor temp is above 45°F. At those temperatures, the primary heater should handle the load alone. Often indicates clogged filter, refrigerant leak, or failing compressor. Estimated waste: $15-20/month.
Filter Degradation — Tracks total HVAC runtime since last filter change. When runtime exceeds 200 hours AND aux heater usage trends up, it alerts the homeowner. Dirty filter restricts airflow, forcing backup heater use at 2-3x the cost.
Connectivity Loss — Detects when thermostat goes offline. Reports last known state (mode, temps) so homeowner knows conditions. Severity: CRITICAL — offline thermostat can't respond to temperature changes or DR events.
💡 Real-World Application: On a real Snyder thermostat, these detectors would analyze actual telemetry events. The physics would be real — not simulated — but the anomaly detection logic and AI integration would work identically.

💰 Pillar 2: Energy Bill Explainability

Correlates HVAC runtime with real weather data to explain why energy costs are what they are. Instead of just showing a number, the system tells you why your bill is high and what's driving the cost.

It explains that a 95°F day with high humidity forces the AC to run longer, or that a cold snap triggered expensive auxiliary heating.

🌿 Pillar 3: Demand Response & Clean Energy Advisor

Prepares for demand response events, explains PRISM impact, and suggests pre-cooling/heating strategies to maintain comfort while reducing grid load during peak demand.

Frequency: 3-4 events per simulated week (Mon, Wed, Thu + occasional Fri) during afternoon peak hours (2-5 PM), matching real-world DR program patterns.

⚡ PRISM Integration: In production, the DR advisor would integrate with PRISM (Proactive Resource and Intelligent Scheduling Manager) to align thermostat behavior with grid carbon intensity — running AC harder when the grid is clean (solar/wind) and reducing load when it's dirty (coal/gas).
